An alt Decision Tree

The image

  • contain text

    and

    • the text in the image is also present as real text nearby.
    • the text is only shown for visual effects.
    • the text in the image is not present otherwise.
  • used in a link or a button, and would it be hard or impossible to understand what the link or the button does, if the image wasn’t there?
  • contribute meaning to the current page or context?

    and

    • it’s a simple graphic or photograph.
    • it’s a graph or complex piece of information.
    • it shows content that is redundant to real text nearby.
  • purely decorative or not intended for the user?
  • use not listed above or it’s unclear what alt text to provide?
